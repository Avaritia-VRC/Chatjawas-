Famous Silvia generations

Silvia S13

Produced from 1988 to 1994, the S13 is iconic for its boxy wedge shape and was a favorite among drifters worldwide.

Silvia S14

The S14 1993–1998 with two facelift one called zenki and the thoers called kouki, this one featured more aggressive body and improved suspension — a modern update to the Silvia line.

Silvia S15

Launched in 1999, the S15 featured a more angular design and the SR20DET engine, solidifying its legend status.
